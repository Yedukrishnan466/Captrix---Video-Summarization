About Captrix

Captrix is an AI-powered video summarization platform designed to transform long-form video content into concise, structured, and meaningful summaries — helping users save time and enhance productivity.

The Vision Behind Captrix

In today’s digital era, video content dominates education and communication. However, watching lengthy videos consumes valuable time.

Captrix was developed to intelligently extract key insights using Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing techniques.

Our goal is simple: deliver maximum knowledge in minimum time.

How Captrix Works

The summarization pipeline follows a structured AI workflow:

  • Upload: User uploads or links a video.
  • Speech Analysis: Audio is processed and transcribed.
  • NLP Processing: Key points are extracted.
  • Smart Summary: Structured output is generated instantly.
